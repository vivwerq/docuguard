Under the Hood
Four independent signals.
One clear verdict.

We run every document through four orthogonal forensic layers simultaneously. No single signal makes the call — all four must agree.

01
Error Level Analysis

Re-compresses the image and maps regions with anomalous JPEG artifacts — edits leave a distinctive brightness signature.

02
Metadata Integrity

Deep EXIF/XMP inspection for timestamp inconsistencies, missing fields, and software signatures that reveal photo editing tools.

03
Noise Fingerprint

Analyzes sensor noise patterns at pixel level. Copy-pasted or AI-generated regions have statistically different noise profiles.

04
Layout Consistency

Template-matches against standard Indian ID aspect ratios and edge contours to detect bad crops, screenshots, or structural deviations.
